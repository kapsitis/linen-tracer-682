Quizzes

Quizzes were used to provide some instant feedback activity during the lectures. The tests were done in Socrative - it allows to fill in the tests using mobile devices as well. Most of the test questions were multiple choice; some were also short answers (positive integers and such).

Most quizzes did not affect the grade (about 3 questions for each theory topic) - but the answer was evaluated immediately after submitting each question. Midterms and finals were accompanied by longer quizzes. They were worth 4 percents of the total grade each. Meanwhile, the essay part of the midterm and the final were 16% each.
